ABOUT US...!!

With the aim to power the local, Hashstar was started with a vision to improve the market. The trend of online in 2010 but the local market still remains offline. The local markets have an unrealized potential to serve the customers better. The local shops and sellers can fulfill local needs easily and swiftly. Hashstar enables these local sellers with a platform to take their shops online. Sellers can sell goods and services online to their local customers. Online Selling depends on the appearance of the product in product images. To provide e-commerce standard images, Hashstar also provides sellers with cataloging and description services. Hashstar also aims at providing the fastest delivery in your own city. Order anything from your local market and get it delivered in just 2 hours. Company was established in 2017, by Akshay M. Hood and Shubham A. Mesre, who were both in academics at the time. The team has since become more experienced and self taught on various aspects of business and the market.
